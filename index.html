About Us

Welcome to KrishiMitra, your go-to platform for contract farming solutions. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between farmers and buyers through a streamlined and transparent contract farming system.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers by providing them with a reliable marketplace for their produce. By offering secure and transparent contract farming agreements, we aim to stabilize incomes, reduce market risks, and foster long-term relationships between farmers and buyers.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every farmer has access to a stable and predictable income through guaranteed contracts, and where buyers can easily source high-quality produce from trusted suppliers. Our goal is to create a more equitable and efficient agricultural supply chain.

What We Do

KrishiMitra connects farmers with buyers through an intuitive online platform that facilitates:

  • Contract Management: Our platform enables farmers and buyers to create and manage contracts with clear terms, reducing misunderstandings and disputes.
  • Price Negotiation: We provide tools for fair and transparent price negotiations, ensuring that both parties agree on a price that reflects the value of the produce.
  • Secure Payment Processing: Timely and secure payments are crucial for maintaining trust. Our platform ensures that farmers receive payments promptly upon delivery of their produce.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We believe in transparent and honest dealings. Our platform is designed to foster trust and reliability between all parties.
  • Innovation: We are committed to continuously improving our services and leveraging technology to meet the evolving needs of farmers and buyers.
  • Empowerment: Our focus is on empowering farmers by giving them control over their contracts and market access, leading to sustainable livelihoods.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to promoting sustainable agricultural practices that benefit both the environment and the farming community.

Our Team

Our team comprises professionals with extensive experience in agriculture, technology, and business management. We are passionate about making a positive impact in the agricultural sector and are committed to supporting farmers and buyers in achieving their goals.
